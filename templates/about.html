About Corn Leaf Diagnostic

An AI-powered tool to identify corn leaf diseases quickly and provide actionable recommendations.

Why Corn Leaf Diagnostic?

Corn Leaf Diagnostic is designed to help farmers and agricultural experts detect common corn leaf diseases such as Blight, Common Rust, and Gray Leaf Spot using a simple image upload. By leveraging advanced deep learning models like ResNet50, the tool provides accurate predictions along with confidence levels.

With real-time recommendations based on the disease type, users can take immediate actions to protect their crops, reduce losses, and improve yield.

Key Features

AI-Powered Diagnosis

Uses state-of-the-art neural networks to accurately classify corn leaf diseases.

Instant Recommendations

Provides actionable advice for controlling disease spread and maintaining crop health.

User-Friendly Interface

Easily upload images, view predictions, and follow recommended actions without technical expertise.
