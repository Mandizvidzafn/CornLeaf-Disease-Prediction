About The AI Powered Corn Leaf Diagnostic App

Detect diseases in corn leaves quickly using AI & ML image classification and get actionable recommendations.

What The App Does

This application uses a deep learning model (ResNet50) trained on corn leaf images to detect diseases like Blight, Common Rust, and Gray Leaf Spot. It gives farmers or agronomists a confidence score and actionable steps to manage the disease efficiently.

How It Works

1. Upload Leaf Image

Users upload a photo of a corn leaf in PNG or JPEG format.

2. Preprocessing

The image is resized and normalized to match the AI model's input format.

3. AI Prediction

The model analyzes the image and predicts the disease class: Blight, Rust, Gray Leaf Spot, or Healthy.

4. Results & Recommendations

Displays the predicted disease, confidence score, and actionable steps for disease management.
